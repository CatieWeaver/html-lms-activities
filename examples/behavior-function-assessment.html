Understanding why a behavior occurs (its function) is essential for developing effective interventions. This tool uses principles from Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) and Positive Behavioral Interventions and Supports (PBIS) to help you identify behavioral functions and match them with evidence-based strategies.

You'll identify the type of challenging behavior, assess its likely function (what the behavior accomplishes for the student), and receive practical intervention strategies tailored to that function. Treat your result as a working hypothesis: behavior can serve more than one function, and confirming function in practice involves direct observation over time, not a single judgment call.
